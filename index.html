Discover Hotel Liyera in the heart of Sri Lanka, where modern luxury meets the island's rich heritage. Whether you're unwinding by the infinity pool or savoring authentic Sri Lankan cuisine, every corner of Hotel Liyera promises a unique and memorable experience.

Accommodation

Our hotel rooms are relatively spacious, with higher ceilings than average hotels in Bangkok at the same level. Rooms with balconies are a great choice for couples or small families. Our accommodation is catered for both business and leisure.
